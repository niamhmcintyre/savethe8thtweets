Who wants to Save the 8th?

The 8th amendment to the Irish Constitution guarantees the 'right to life of the unborn', and effectively criminalises abortion in all but the most exceptional of crircumstances. A movement to repeal the 8th has received international attention: but who are those campaigning to save the 8th?

#savethe8th Tweets

We scraped every tweet containing '#savethe8th', and it's clear that @lifeinstitute, Ireland's biggest pro-life lobby group, have been using the hashtag more than anyone else.

Let's take a look at the Life Institute's followers twitter bios:

What we might have expected from their bios: Catholic, God, Conservative and Christian were the top key words. The Catholic Church has been essential in organising a pro-8th movement in Ireland, hoping that Pope Francis' visit in 2018 will galvanise opinion in favour. What is less expected: student and students were the 6th and 24th most frequently used terms respectively, with 103 mentions in total. Ireland was mentioned 71 times, to England's 10.
